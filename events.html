3 on 3 Basketball Tournament

On July 11th & 12th 2020, the Step Back Foundation held a 3 on 3 tournament at Allen Park, in North Wildwood. This money will be used towards providing athletic equipment, gift cards, clothes, etc for our local athletes, and our “Live Like Bolle” middle school and high scholarships.

Age Groups Included:
- 5th & 6th grade
- 7th & 8th grade
- 9th - 12th grade
- Adults (Anyone 18 or older)

“Live Like Bolle” 5K Island Race

On Saturday, August 8th, 2020, the Step Back Foundation hosted our second annual, “Live Like Bolle” 5K run and a 1-mile walk, on the beach in North Wildwood. This is where Mr. Bill Bolle spent most of his summer days. This year, we had a total of 180 runners/walkers (110 runners and 70 walkers).

This race was dedicated and in memory of Mr. Bill Bolle, who tragically passed away in October of 2017. Mr. Bolle represented kindness, positive energy, and light, which was displayed throughout the entire race.

Keenan’s Virtual Palooza

Over Easter weekend, April 11th & 12th, Keenan’s Irish Pub hosted over 15 virtual performances, taking in donations. Throughout the weekend, Keenan’s and these bands raised over $8,000 for the Step Back Foundation. The money raised has been going towards purchasing and delivering Acme gift cards to Wildwood families.

We cannot thank Keenan’s & all of their performances enough. Stay tuned to Keenan’s and their opening for the summer.
